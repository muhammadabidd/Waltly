FAQ
Questions, answered.
Does Waltly connect to my bank? +
No — and that's by design. Indonesian banks don't offer clean developer APIs, and screen-scraping is fragile and risky. You log transactions manually (2 taps via widget), so your money data never leaves your phone.
Is it really one-time payment? +
Yes. Pay Rp 79,000 once and Pro is yours forever — on every iOS device signed in with your Apple ID. No renewal, no surprise charges, no feature held hostage by a subscription.
Do you support Android? +
Not yet. Waltly is built iOS-first to take full advantage of widgets, Control Center, StandBy, and SwiftUI polish. Android is on the roadmap for 2027.
Can I export my data? +
Absolutely. Pro users can export full transaction history as CSV at any time. Your data is yours — if you ever leave, you walk out with everything.
What languages are supported? +
Bahasa Indonesia and English at launch. Every label, tooltip, and copy string is written natively in both — not machine-translated. More languages coming based on user votes.
